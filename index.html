About Us

We are a leading manufacturer, trader, importer and supplier of a wide range of adhesive tapes and packing materials. Our product offerings include adhesive tapes such as carton packing tape in clear and brown, Scotch tape, paper gummed tape, automotive-grade masking tape, double-sided tissue tape, and double-sided OPP tape, among others. Our packing materials include plastic bags (polythene, HD poly bags, PP poly bags), PP strapping rolls, stretch film rolls, plastic dori, bubble wrap rolls and customized bubble pouches to meet specific needs.
